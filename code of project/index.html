DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE



Dr. Abhishek Pandey


I am delighted to introduce the Faculty Research Portal, a platform designed exclusively for our esteemed faculty members and students at Chandigarh University. This portal is a one-stop destination for all research-related activities, aimed at promoting and enhancing the research culture at our institution.
The Faculty Research Portal provides a comprehensive list of research opportunities and funding sources, as well as a repository of research papers, publications, and other relevant resources. This platform will enable our faculty members to collaborate with researchers from other institutions, both nationally and internationally, fostering a culture of innovation and excellence.
At Chandigarh University, we recognize the pivotal role that research plays in the growth and development of our institution. Through the Faculty Research Portal, we aim to provide our faculty members with the necessary resources and support to engage in cutting-edge research activities, that will contribute to the development of our institution, and society at large.
I urge all our esteemed faculty members to make the most of this platform and utilize it to its fullest potential. Let us work together towards creating a research-driven institution that is at the forefront of innovation and change..
